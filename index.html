About GITAM

The Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management, commonly known as GITAM, Visakhapatnam is one of the best private engineering Deemed to be University established in 1980. The university has AICTE approval and NAAC A++ accreditation.

The university has collaborations with several reputed international universities. The university also has a vibrant student community with students from all over India and abroad, making it a truly diverse and multicultural environment.

About Nirman

Nirman is the Civil Engineering Club at GITAM University, dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among civil engineering students. The club serves as a platform for students to explore beyond classroom learning and engage in practical applications of civil engineering concepts.

Through workshops, competitions, guest lectures, and industry visits, Nirman aims to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements, preparing students for successful careers in the field of civil engineering.

Our Previous Events

The year 2023 was a milestone for our civil engineering community, highlighted by two remarkable events—Civil Engineering Tech Fest Nirman 2023 and Nirman 2.0. Both events were resounding successes, showcasing innovation, talent, and collaboration, and setting new standards for participation and impact.

Civil Engineering Tech Fest 2023

200+ Participants 8 Colleges

The Civil Engineering Tech Fest 2023 brought together over 200 participants from 8 colleges across the state. This vibrant event featured an exciting lineup of technical and non-technical competitions, offering students a platform to showcase their creativity and expertise.

A key highlight was the guest lecture by civil engineer Ravichandu Baratam, who shared insights from his prestigious projects in India and abroad.

Nirman 2.0

300+ Participants Multiple Competitions

In 2023, we hosted Nirman 2.0, attracting over 300 participants and exceeding expectations. The event featured dynamic competitions and engaging activities, highlighting our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Nirman 2.0 successfully bridged academia and industry for a collaborative and enriching experience, setting a new benchmark for future events.
